About me

I'm Yelyzaveta, but you can call me Elya. My hometown is Dnepr - beautiful city combines fast-growing industrial areas with freshness of parks and breathtaking views over the river. In 2019 I left my hometown to begin a journey of reinventing myself in a big world. I traveled to Canada to take a first step in my career - start a Computer Science Coop Bachelor program at Brock University. Getting accustomed to new culture and keeping up with academic deadlines while living alone for the first time was a tough challenge but I will always be grateful for this experience as it made more dedicated than ever. As my graduation looms, I reflect on a path marked by intellectual growth and professional refinement. I have completed several coop work terms enriching my knowledge and sharpening my skills while also greatly expanding my network with brilliant people. However, I could not settle at this and wanted to diversify my journey even more by becoming a research and later teaching assistant at my university. All that not only gave me pricelessly valuable knowledge and skills, but it was simply fun and fulfilling. I was looking forward each day to see how I can advance my research, or what cool feature can I add to my project, or how can I engage students into learning a new concept. As I am approaching my graduation and my time at Brock University is about to end, this is only the beginning of my journey, and I cannot wait to see where it will bring me.
