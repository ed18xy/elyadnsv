About me

This is me. A bright sun and the creator of all energy around.
My name is Yelyzaveta Denysova or Elya for friends, and I am from a lovely but busy city Dnepr, Ukraine. In 2019 I left my hometown to begin a journey of discovering myself in a big world. I traveled to Canada to start a Computer Science Coop Bachelor program at Brock University. Getting used to new culture and keeping up with studies while living alone for the first time was a tough challenge but I will always be grateful for this experience as it made more dedicated than ever.

Inspiration

This is my inspiration planet. Here I grow things that brings joy and motivation to everything I do.
The biggest inspiration for my programming career is my high school IT teacher. Not only he introduced me to coding but showed how fun and cool it is. I made my first video game in grade 8 and it felt like a discovery of a new lifeform in space. It was simple and not that good looking but having something on hands, that I could actually play, after months of work was the greatest reward.
I was always interested in learning about universe and space but even more exciting for me is to read about what could be in space. Science fiction is my favourite genre and I especially enjoy The Martian Chronicles by Ray Bradbury. I like imagining what kind of life can exist somewhere around us. If you're not a fan of big books, I highly recommend his collection of short stories.
I can't go without mentioning one of my favourite sources of creative mood - dancing. It goes without saying that I love music too.
