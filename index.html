About me

This is me. A bright sun and the creator of all energy around.
My name is Yelyzaveta Denysova or Elya for friends. My hometown is Dnepr - beautiful city combines fast-growing industrial areas with freshness of parks and breathtaking views over the river. In 2019 I left my hometown to begin a journey of reinventing myself in a big world. I traveled to Canada to take a first step in my career ladder - start a Computer Science Coop Bachelor program at Brock University. Getting accustomed to new culture and keeping up with academic deadlines while living alone for the first time was a tough challenge but I will always be grateful for this experience as it made more dedicated than ever.
